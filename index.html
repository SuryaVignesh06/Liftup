Engineering Tracks

One Program. Four Engineering Tracks.

Colleges choose the track that fits their students best. Every track runs inside the same 15-day corporate simulation — same sprint structure, same evaluation, different product focus.

Track ABest for: CSE, IT

AI-First Web Engineering

Full-stack development with AI embedded at every layer. Students build and deploy production-ready web applications using AI copilots, automated UI generation, and backend automation tools.

Track BBest for: AI / ML specialization

AI & Agent Engineering

For students going deep into AI systems. Teams build LLM-powered features, autonomous task pipelines, multi-agent systems, and RAG applications that solve real business problems.

Track CBest for: MBA Tech, Product streams

AI for Startup & Product Engineering

Rapid MVP development using AI, no-code and low-code hybrid systems, and SaaS product building. Built for students who want to think like founders, not just engineers.

Track DBest for: Data Science, Analytics

Data & Applied AI Engineering

Data pipelines, real-time analytics dashboards, vector databases, and AI microservices. For students who want to work at the intersection of data and production AI systems.

Not sure which track fits your institution? Our team will help you map the right track to your students' stream and goals.
