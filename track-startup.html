Track C • 15 Day Bootcamp

AI for STARTUP &
PRODUCT ENGINEERING.

Stop just building features. Start building validated businesses with AI.

Program Goal

Enable founders and builders to rapidly design, validate, and launch AI-powered SaaS products using AI-assisted development, no-code/low-code tools, and scalable product infrastructure.

The Journey

15 Days from Idea to Launch.

01
Ideation
Days 1–3
D1

AI Startup Landscape

Identify niches and categories in the AI ecosystem. Perform problem-first design to find high-value pain points.

Outcome: Validated Problem Statement
D2

AI-Assisted Validation

Perform market research and competitor analysis using AI tools. Test your value proposition against market data.

D3

MVP Scope & Roadmap

Define your Minimum Viable Product. Prioritize features using AI to balance complexity and speed.

02
Rapid MVP
Days 4–6
D4

No-Code/Low-Code Shell

Build your product architecture using modern no-code platforms. Focus on rapid prototyping and user flow.

D5

Custom AI Integration

Integrate LLM capabilities into your product shell. Build custom prompts and AI components for your core features.

D6

Lean UI/UX Design

Design high-converting landing pages and intuitive dashboards. Optimize for user experience and retention.

03
Validation
Days 7–9
D7

Market Validation Pipelines

Set up tracking for demand signals. Perform "fake door" tests and collect early waitlist data.

D8

SaaS Analytics & Growth

Implement behavior tracking with PostHog or Mixpanel. Learn to measure product-market fit metrics.

D9

AI-Driven Feedback

Use AI to summarize user interviews and survey results. Iterate on your product based on automated insights.

04
Infrastructure
Days 10–12
D10

Scalable Foundations

Set up your production database and authentication systems. Ensure your MVP can handle early growth.

D11

Subscriptions & Revenue

Integrate Stripe for recurring billing. Implement subscription tiers and automated invoicing.

D12

AI Support Systems

Build AI-powered customer success bots. Automate support workflows to scale your startup operations.

05
Growth & Launch
Days 13–15
D13

Growth Automation

Implement AI-driven marketing and SEO strategies. Automate lead generation and content pipelines.

D14

Launch Ready

Finalize your pitch deck and launch strategy. Prepare your Product Hunt launch and marketing creative.

D15

Demo Day & Pitch

Present your working AI SaaS MVP to mentors and founders. Graduate as a product-ready founder.

Capstone: MVP Showcase & Founder Pitch
